Who we are

Established in 1978, Enppi Library serves as an information center to serve engineering and energy sectors. The library supports both the organization and the public through curated collections, digital services and learning spaces with global information resources.

What we do

  • Provide a wide range of academic readings
  • Offer audio books and cultural materials
  • Host events, talks and workshops
  • Support e‑learning and research platforms
  • Maintain academic & research projects database
